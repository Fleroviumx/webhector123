Kingsman Tailor is a formal attire brand and online retailer based in Australia. With customers in 69 countries around the world, Kingsman Tailor has sparked a global movement within the attire industry, to make sustainable thinking the standard, not the exception.

Kingsman Tailor is led by it's co-founders, meng-gg and meng-gokil, who formed the attire company in 2069, with the aim of creating stunning, high-performance suit products that do good for the environment.

Kingsman Tailor is underpinned by 3 key principles:

Design.

Sustainability.

Performance.
